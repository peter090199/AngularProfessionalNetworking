Data Protection Policy

This Data Protection Policy (“Policy”) sets out the basis on which Nexsuz (“we” or “us”) may collect, use, disclose, or otherwise process the personal data of our platform users in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (“PDPA”). This Policy applies to personal data in our possession or under our control, including personal data in the possession of individuals or organizations we have engaged to collect, use, disclose, or process personal data for our purposes.

1. Personal Data
As used in this Policy: “user” refers to an individual or organization who (a) voluntarily accesses, signs up, or creates an account on our platform, (b) contacts us to learn more about any of our services, or (c) enters into a contract with us for the supply of services. “Personal data” means data or information, whether true or not, about a user who can be identified from that data or from that data and other information to which we have or are likely to have access. Depending on the nature of your interaction with us, some examples of personal data which we may collect from you include your name, identification information, contact details, nationality, gender, date of birth, marital status, photographs, audio-visual information, education, employment information, and financial information such as credit card or bank account details.

2. Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Data
We generally do not collect your personal data unless it is provided to us voluntarily by you directly or via a third party who is authorized by you. We may collect and use your personal data for purposes such as performing services requested by you, verifying your identity, responding to queries, managing our relationship with you, processing payments, sending marketing information, complying with laws, and other purposes directly related to our business operations. We may disclose your personal data where necessary to perform services requested by you or to third-party service providers, agents, or organizations engaged by us.

3. Withdrawing Your Consent
The consent you provide for the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data will remain valid until withdrawn in writing. You may withdraw consent by submitting a request to our Data Protection Officer at ndpo@nexsuz.com. We will inform you of any consequences of withdrawing consent, including potential impact on our ability to provide services.

4. Access to and Correction of Personal Data
To request access to or correction of your personal data, please submit a written request to our Data Protection Officer. We may charge a reasonable fee for access requests and will inform you of any fee before processing.

5. Protection of Personal Data
We take measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or other risks. However, while we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Accuracy of Personal Data
We rely on personal data provided by you or your authorized representative. Please update us if there are changes to your personal data by contacting our Data Protection Officer.

7. Retention of Personal Data
We retain your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by law. Once retention is no longer necessary, we will remove or anonymize your data.

8. Transfers of Personal Data Outside of the Philippines
We generally do not transfer your personal data outside of the Philippines. If required, we will obtain your consent and ensure your data receives comparable protection.

9. Contacting Our Data Protection Officer
For inquiries about our data protection policies or to make a request, contact our Data Protection Officer at ndpo@nexsuz.com.

10. Effect of Policy and Changes
This Policy applies in conjunction with any other notices or consent clauses regarding personal data. We may revise this Policy without prior notice. Continued use of our services signifies your acceptance of such changes.
